ADDING CAR WITH IMAGE:
1. Login as admin
2. Go to "Manage Cars" â†’ "Add New Car"
3. Fill in all car details
4. Click "Choose File" under "Car Image"
5. Select image (JPG, PNG, or GIF)

7. Click "Add Car"

UPDATING CAR IMAGE:
1. Login as admin
2. Go to "Manage Cars"
3. Click edit icon on any car
4. See current image (if exists)
5. Click "Choose File" to change
6. Or leave empty to keep current
7. Click "Update Car"
8. Old image deleted, new one uploaded!
